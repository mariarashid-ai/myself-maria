About

My name is Maria Rashid, and I am a passionate Computer Science student. I am deeply interested in technology and solving real-world problems through programming. I am always eager to learn new skills and explore emerging technologies. With a focus on web development and Artificial Intelligence, I strive to create innovative solutions that make a difference.
